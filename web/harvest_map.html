🍵 Matcha Harvest Map

When and where ceremonial-grade matcha is picked — plus what it costs right now.

Early Apr → late May
Ichibancha (first flush) — the only flush most producers shade and grind into ceremonial matcha — moves north across Japan from Kagoshima to Saitama. Everything later in the year is lower-grade or not matcha at all.

Bars in color are graded into matcha; gray bars are the same plants' later pickings, sold as bancha/hojicha or bulk leaf instead. The underline marks a rule-based "good time to buy" window — see note below.

"Good time to buy" is a rule derived from the sourced harvest calendar (fresh-crop window + autumn kuradashi release where documented) — not a model fit on price history.
